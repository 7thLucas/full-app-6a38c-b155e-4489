# Product Overview — We Love Meepo x QuantumByte

## What it is
A single-page, single-section text-only landing page. One bold declaration: **"We Love Meepo x QuantumByte"**. No images, no navigation, no forms — pure typographic statement.

## Audience
Anyone directed to the URL — fans, community members, or anyone celebrating the Meepo x QuantumByte collaboration.

## Positioning
A celebration / tribute page. Minimal by design. The message is the product.

## Brand & Tone
- **Tone**: Bold, celebratory, direct
- **Style**: Text-first, stripped back, confident
- **Palette**: Dark background, strong contrasting headline type (owner may adjust)

## Scope
- 1 page
- 1 section
- Text only: the phrase "We Love Meepo x QuantumByte"
- No navigation, no footer, no forms, no images

## Strategic Principles
- Less is more — the single headline IS the experience
- No distractions from the core message
- Fast to load, easy to share

## Out of Scope
- Multi-section layouts
- Images or media
- Interactive elements
- User accounts or data collection
